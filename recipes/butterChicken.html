Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken! The ultimate comfort food your entire family will love! Make the best flavour-packed, creamy and delicious authentic Indian Butter Chicken at home with this easy recipe.

Butter Chicken also known as Chicken Makhani is a classic Indian ish that's made by simmering marinate and grilled chicken in a spicy, aromatic, buttery and creamy tomato gravy. This lip-smacking and delicious dish is hugely popular among the Indian food lovers across the world.

  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Tumeric
